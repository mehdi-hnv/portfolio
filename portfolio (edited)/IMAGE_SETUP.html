Required Image Files

1. profile.jpg

Used on: Home page (Hero section)

Recommended Size: 400x400px or larger

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: Your professional profile photo. Will be displayed as a circular image with a glowing neon border.

Tip: Use a high-quality photo with good lighting and a simple background.

2. about-photo.jpg

Used on: About page (Right side)

Recommended Size: 500x600px

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: A personal photo for your about page. Can be a different photo showing you at work or in action.

Tip: Keep aspect ratio around 3:4 for best display.

3. project1-screenshot.jpg

Used on: Projects page (First featured project)

Recommended Size: 800x450px (16:9 aspect ratio)

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: A screenshot or demo image of your first major project.

Tip: Show your project in action. Include UI elements or features.

4. project2-screenshot.jpg

Used on: Projects page (Second featured project - Hour of Code)

Recommended Size: 800x450px (16:9 aspect ratio)

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: A screenshot of your Hour of Code project or second major project.

Tip: Showcase the most interesting part of your project.

5. project3-thumbnail.jpg

Used on: Projects page (Other projects grid)

Recommended Size: 400x250px

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: Thumbnail for a third project in the grid section.

Tip: Keep thumbnails consistent in size and quality.

6. project4-thumbnail.jpg

Used on: Projects page (Other projects grid)

Recommended Size: 400x250px

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: Thumbnail for a fourth project.

7. project5-thumbnail.jpg

Used on: Projects page (Other projects grid)

Recommended Size: 400x250px

Format: JPG or PNG

Description: Thumbnail for a fifth project.
