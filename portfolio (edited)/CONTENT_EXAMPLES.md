## ‚ú® Writing Tips

### Be Specific, Not Generic
‚ùå "Good at coding"
‚úÖ "Built REST APIs handling 5M+ requests/month"

### Use Action Words
"Built", "Designed", "Implemented", "Optimized", "Scaled", "Launched", 
"Architected", "Refactored", "Deployed", "Integrated"

### Include Numbers
- User counts
- Performance improvements
- Uptime percentages
- Transaction volumes
- Load reduction percentages
- Time savings

### Show Impact
- What problem did you solve?
- How many people benefited?
- What was the business outcome?
- What did you learn?

### Be Honest
- Don't claim skills you don't have
- Mention both wins and lessons learned
- Be realistic about your experience level
- Show growth and willingness to learn
